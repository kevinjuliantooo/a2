Goals:

  • To recreate Fish-Tastic's original website in a systematic way with clear CTA and improved visual effects
  • To reconstruct Fish-Tastic in order to promote E-business

    • Success Evaluation:

  • There is at least total of 1000 customers get in touch with Fish-tastic through email/contact number/walk-in
  • There is more than 200 people participate in our event
  • There is at least 500 orders and online payments from customers
  • There is at least 1000 viewers that browse through Fish-Tastic website

    • Target Audience:

    Young adults and adults (Ages 18 to 60), interior designers and fish experts.

    We will not use cartoon or kids based design, we provide clear CTA for interested buyers and professional design

    Content:

  • About Us : In this page, we display our company information, our goals and visions
  • Our Fish World: This page display all of our fishes that we have in our company, which includes salted water fish aquarium and fresh water fish aquarium
  • Accessories: In this page, we display all the accessories, tool, medical veterinary that is provided in order to support the fish aquarium.
  • Events: In this page, we help the visitors to know what events we are currently holding or have had in the past.
  • Contact Us: In this page, we display our company’s contact details (fax, office phone number…) and FAQs that have been answered overtime before in the past.
  • Online form: This page includes the online form and feedback form for customers to fill in their opinion feedback and take orders.
